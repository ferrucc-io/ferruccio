How to put text files on your TI-84 Plus CE-T

  • Download and Install TI Connect from the Texas Instruments official website.
  • Download this program
  • Connect your calculator to your computer
  • Open TI Connect
  • Click on "Open Program"
  • Add the "ZTEXT" program to your calculator on the RAM
  • Use the converter on this page to generate the file with the text you need and download it.
  • Add the newly created file to the calculator on the RAM

    • Congratulations, you can now read text files on your calculator!
